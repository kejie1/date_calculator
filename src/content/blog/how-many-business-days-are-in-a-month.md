# How Many Business Days Are in a Month?

For human resources directors, project coordinators, and freelance contractors, calculating the exact number of business days (working days) in a single month is vital for structuring payroll, estimating product delivery windows, and mapping task allocations.

## Short Answer
A standard calendar month contains **20 to 23 business working days** (Monday through Friday, excluding Saturdays and Sundays). The exact number fluctuates constantly based on whether the month contains 28, 29, 30, or 31 total calendar days, and which weekday the month begins on. ## Detailed Monthly Distribution

Because our calendar does not have uniform month lengths, business day totals shift throughout the year:

- **February (28-day months):** Standardly contains exactly **20 business days** (comprising exactly 4 full 5-day workweeks, with 8 weekend days).
- **February (29-day leap years):** Contains either **20 or 21 business days**, depending on which weekday February 1st lands on.
- **30-day months (April, June, September, November):** Standardly contain **20 to 22 business days**.
- **31-day months (Jan, Mar, May, Jul, Aug, Oct, Dec):** Standardly contain **21 to 23 business days**.

## FAQ

### Does "business days in a month" include public holidays?
No. Standard working day metrics (such as the standard 20-23 day range) exclude Saturdays and Sundays only. Since national and regional holidays are highly localized, they must be subtracted manually based on your regional office schedules.

### How do payroll departments handle these monthly fluctuations?
Many payroll systems normalize monthly pay by using an average divisor (like 21.67 working days per month) to ensure salary deposits remain predictable regardless of calendar shifts.
